# Joe Schmo Real Estate — SMS Templates for Lead Capture & Nurture

**Important**: Only send to leads who explicitly opted in via the landing page checkbox. Include opt-out language. Goal: First text within 5 minutes of form submission.

**Trigger Rules** (from landing page capture):
- Immediate (0-5 min): Thank you + report delivery
- 30-60 min: Soft follow-up if no reply
- Day 1: Value + question
- Day 3 & 7: Check-in

---

## Immediate SMS (Send within 5 minutes)

**Template 1 (Valuation Delivery)**:
"Hi [First Name], this is Joe Schmo with Joe Schmo Real Estate. Thanks for requesting your free valuation for [Address]! Your report is attached in the email I just sent. Quick question — are you thinking of buying or selling in the next 30-90 days? Text me back and I'll help. - Joe (555) 123-4567"

**Template 2 (If they also opted for texts)**:
"Your free home valuation report for [Address] is ready. Check your email. Want me to text you 3 recent comps in your neighborhood? Just reply YES."

---

## 30-60 Minute Follow-up (if no reply)

"Hi [First Name], Joe here again. Did you get the valuation report for [Address]? Happy to answer any questions about the numbers or the market — no pressure. Text or call anytime."

---

## Day 1 SMS

"Hi [First Name]. Following up on your valuation request. The Patels in Maplewood used the same tool and closed 11 days faster than they expected. Want me to run a similar off-market search for you? Just say the word."

---

## Day 3 SMS (Education / Light Nurture)

"Quick market note for [Neighborhood]: Inventory is up this month, which is good news if you're buying. If selling, pricing right is key right now. Need help with either? I'm around today."

---

## Day 7 SMS (Direct but polite)

"[First Name], it's Joe. It's been a week since your valuation for [Address]. Most folks I help are ready to make a move within 30 days once they see real numbers. Got 5 minutes this week for a quick chat? No obligation."

---

## Hot Lead / Appointment Booking SMS

(Use when they show interest):
"Perfect. I have openings this week for a quick 15-min call or private tour. What works better for you — Tue 4pm, Wed 10am, or Thu evening? Just reply with your choice."

---

## Listing Appointment SMS (Seller leads)

"Hi [First Name]. For the valuation on [Address], I can come by this week to give you a full net proceeds estimate and walk through what selling would actually look like. Thursday or Friday work?"

---

## Buyer Showing SMS

"Great. I have 3 homes that match what you described that aren't on Zillow yet. Want to see them this weekend? I can set up private tours."

---

## Re-engagement (for cold leads after 14-21 days)

"[First Name], Joe with Joe Schmo Real Estate. Just checking in on the valuation you requested. Market is moving — happy to give you an updated number or answer questions. Text STOP to opt out."

---

**Best Practices for Success**:
- Personalize with name and address whenever possible.
- Keep under 160 characters when possible (one text).
- Always include your name and number.
- Use "text me back" language — it increases replies.
- Log every SMS in the tracking sheet (date, type, response).
- Combine with the email sequence for multi-touch (text + email = much higher response).

With fast SMS + the email sequence + the landing page, you should see 15-25% of captured leads turn into conversations, and 30-40% of those book appointments in Tier 2/3 setups.

Copy these directly into your texting platform (ManyChat, SimpleTexting, or even manual for starters).

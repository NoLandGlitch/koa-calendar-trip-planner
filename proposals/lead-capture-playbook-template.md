# [Business Name] Lead Capture Playbook Template

**Goal**: Turn visitors into owned, qualified leads at 12-18% conversion rate, then nurture them into customers without losing them.

## 1. On-Page Capture Best Practices

Current form fields (customize for your industry):
- Name
- Phone (very important for local/service businesses)
- Email
- [Specific qualifying question – e.g. Zip, Company Size, Main Challenge, Budget Range]
- [Lead Type dropdown – e.g. Buyer/Seller, Residential/Commercial, New/Existing Customer]
- Timeline

**Recommended Optimizations**:
- Keep the form short (4 fields max for initial capture).
- Add instant value promise: "Your free [Lead Magnet] will be emailed in under 60 seconds + texted to your phone."
- Strong CTA: "Get My Free [Lead Magnet] →"
- Trust elements above the form using your [Social Proof].
- Add SMS opt-in checkbox.
- Exit-intent popup with the same offer.
- Mobile-first design.

## 2. Multi-Channel Traffic Sources

Drive people to your landing page using these channels (ranked for most businesses):

1. **[Primary Channel for your industry]** — e.g. Nextdoor for local services, LinkedIn for B2B, Facebook Groups for consumer.
2. Local Facebook/Community Groups or industry-specific groups.
3. Google Business Profile / Local SEO.
4. Email signature + past client nurture.
5. Referrals ("Know anyone else who needs [your service]? Send them this link.").
6. Paid ads (retargeting + search) once you have initial traffic.

Target: 200-400 visitors/week to start.

## 3. Immediate Capture + Delivery

- Instant on-screen thank you with clear delivery promise.
- Auto-respond via email + SMS (use the template sequences).
- Deliver the actual [Lead Magnet] (PDF, checklist, guide, audit, etc.).
- Tag the lead in your CRM with source and qualification data.

## 4. Nurture Automation

Most leads are not ready immediately. Automated follow-up is where real capture happens.

- **Email Nurture Sequence**: See `email-nurture-sequence-template.md`
- **SMS Sequence**: See `sms-templates-template.md`

**Key rule**: First contact (preferably SMS) within 5 minutes. Then email within 30 minutes.

## 5. Qualification & Speed to Lead

- Use form fields to score leads automatically.
- Auto-assign hot leads to you immediately.
- Goal: Respond to every lead in under 10 minutes during business hours.
- Log time from capture to first contact in the tracking sheet.

## 6. Tracking & Optimization

Use the provided tracking sheet template. Review weekly:
- Capture rate = leads / visitors (aim for 12%+)
- Speed to lead
- Conversion to appointment / sale
- ROI by source

Run A/B tests on headlines, CTA copy, form length, and offer.

## 7. Compliance

- Get explicit SMS opt-in.
- Include "Reply STOP to unsubscribe".
- Follow TCPA rules for calls/texts.
- Link to privacy policy on the landing page.

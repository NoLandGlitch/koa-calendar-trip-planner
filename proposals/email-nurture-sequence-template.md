# [Business Name] — Email Nurture Sequence Template

**Purpose**: Automatically nurture leads captured from the landing page [Lead Magnet] form into customers.

**Setup**: Copy each email into your email tool. Trigger on form submission. Use merge tags.

**Timing**:
- Email 1: Immediate (within 5-15 minutes)
- Email 2: Day 1 (24 hours later)
- Email 3: Day 3
- Email 4: Day 7

---

## Email 1: Instant Delivery + Welcome

**Subject**: Your free [Lead Magnet] for [Specific Detail] is ready, [First Name]

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for requesting your free [Lead Magnet].

I've attached / linked your personalized [Lead Magnet] for [Specific Detail]. It includes:
- [Key benefit 1]
- [Key benefit 2]
- [Key benefit 3]

**Quick question**: Are you looking to [solve the main pain point] in the next 30-90 days?

Just reply to this email or text me at [Phone] and I'll give you straight answers with no pressure.

Looking forward to helping,
[Your Name]
[Business Name]
[Phone]

---

## Email 2: Value + Social Proof

**Subject**: How [Client Type] got [specific result] using our [Lead Magnet]

Hi [First Name],

Yesterday I helped [Client Type] achieve [specific result].

They used the exact same [Lead Magnet] you just received.

Would you like me to run the same customized insights for your situation?

Just reply "yes" or tell me more about your [timeline / challenge].

[Your Name]

---

## Email 3: Education + Soft CTA

**Subject**: [Industry] update for [Service Area] — what [Target Customer] need to know

Hi [First Name],

Here's what I'm seeing right now in [Service Area / Industry]:

- [Insight 1]
- [Insight 2]
- [Insight 3]

If you're ready to [take action], the [Lead Magnet] you requested is a great starting point. Want me to walk you through what it would actually mean for you?

Reply with your situation and I'll send the right next step.

[Your Name]

---

## Email 4: Urgency + Direct Ask

**Subject**: [First Name], should we talk about your [goal] this week?

Hi [First Name],

It's been a week since you grabbed your [Lead Magnet] for [Specific Detail].

Most people I work with who are serious about [solving the pain point] make a move within 30-60 days once they have real information.

I'm not here to pressure you — but I am here to give you straight answers.

Would you like to:
A) Get a customized next-step plan
B) See [examples or options]
C) Just get answers to 2-3 specific questions

Pick A, B, or C and I'll handle the rest. Or reply with a time that works for a quick 10-minute call.

[Your Name]
[Phone]

---

**Pro Tips**:
- Personalize using form data.
- Add photos or examples relevant to your industry.
- Track engagement and log "engaged" leads.
- If no reply after Email 4, move to light monthly value content.

This sequence converts well across many industries when paired with fast SMS follow-up.

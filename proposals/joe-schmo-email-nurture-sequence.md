# Joe Schmo Real Estate — 4-Email Nurture Sequence

**Purpose**: Automatically nurture leads captured from the landing page valuation form into booked showings or listings.

**Setup**: Copy each email into your email tool (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, etc.). Trigger on form submission. Use merge tags for personalization.

**Timing**:
- Email 1: Immediate (within 5-15 minutes of capture)
- Email 2: Day 1 (24 hours later)
- Email 3: Day 3
- Email 4: Day 7

---

## Email 1: Instant Delivery + Welcome (Highest open rate)

**Subject**: Your free valuation report for [Address] is ready, [First Name]

**Body**:

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for requesting your free home valuation.

I've attached your personalized report for [Address]. It includes:
- Current estimated market value range
- Recent comparable sales in your neighborhood
- 2026 market trends for Metro Heights

**Quick question**: Are you thinking about selling in the next 30-90 days, or are you more interested in buying?

Just reply to this email or text me directly at (555) 123-4567 and I'll give you straight answers with no pressure.

In the meantime, here are 3 homes that just hit the market this week that might interest you (if you're a buyer): [link to featured section or separate email].

Looking forward to helping,
Joe Schmo
Joe Schmo Real Estate
(555) 123-4567

P.S. The report is also texted to your phone if you opted in for SMS.

**Pro Tips**:
- Personalize [Address] and [First Name] using your ESP.
- Add 1-2 photos of recent closings or the specific homes mentioned.
- Track opens/clicks and log "engaged" leads in the Google Sheet.
- If no reply after Email 4, move to light monthly value emails + occasional market updates.

This sequence has converted well in similar suburban markets (12-22% of captured leads book an appointment within 14 days when followed up with SMS too).

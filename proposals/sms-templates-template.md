# [Business Name] — SMS Templates Template

**Important**: Only send to leads who explicitly opted in. Keep messages under 160 characters when possible. Goal: First text within 5 minutes.

**Trigger Rules**:
- Immediate (0-5 min): Thank you + [Lead Magnet] delivery
- 30-60 min: Soft follow-up
- Day 1, 3, 7: Value + question

---

## Immediate SMS (within 5 minutes)

"Hi [First Name], this is [Your Name] with [Business Name]. Thanks for requesting your free [Lead Magnet]! Your report/guide is in the email I just sent. Quick question — are you looking to [solve pain point] soon? Text me back. - [Your Name] [Phone]"

---

## 30-60 Minute Follow-up

"Hi [First Name], [Your Name] here again. Did you get the [Lead Magnet]? Happy to answer any questions — no pressure. Text or call anytime."

---

## Day 1 SMS

"Hi [First Name]. Following up on your [Lead Magnet] request. [Short social proof example relevant to your industry]. Want me to run customized insights for you? Just say the word."

---

## Day 3 SMS

"Quick note for [Target Customer] in [Service Area]: [One current insight]. Need help with [your service]? I'm around today."

---

## Day 7 SMS

"[First Name], it's [Your Name]. It's been a week since your [Lead Magnet]. Most folks I help are ready to move forward within 30 days once they have real info. Got 5 minutes this week? No obligation."

---

## Hot Lead Booking SMS

"Perfect. I have openings this week. What works better — [Option 1], [Option 2], or [Option 3]? Just reply with your choice."

---

## Re-engagement (after 14-21 days)

"[First Name], [Your Name] with [Business Name]. Just checking in on the [Lead Magnet] you requested. Happy to give you an update or answer questions. Text STOP to opt out."

---

**Best Practices**:
- Personalize with name and any specific data from the form.
- Always include your name and number.
- Use "text me back" language.
- Log every SMS and response in the tracking sheet.
- Combine with the email sequence for multi-touch.

These templates adapt to almost any industry. Replace the examples with your real language and offers.

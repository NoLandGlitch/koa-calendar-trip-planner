# Universal Lead Generation Template – Instructions for Any Business

**Created via Eye Console Workflow**  
This package turns the Joe Schmo Real Estate deliverables into a reusable system for **any business** (HVAC, real estate, consulting, e-commerce, coaching, home services, professional services, etc.).

## Quick Start
1. Copy the files in this folder.
2. Replace every `[PLACEHOLDER]` with your actual details (see list below).
3. Customize the landing page HTML (open in browser to test).
4. Set up the email/SMS sequences in your tools (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ManyChat, etc.).
5. Drive traffic using the playbook.
6. Log everything in the tracking sheet.

## All Placeholders to Replace
- `[Business Name]` – e.g., Joe Schmo Real Estate, Apex Plumbing, Brightpaw Coaching
- `[Your Name]` – Owner or main contact
- `[Industry]` – Real Estate, Plumbing, Digital Marketing, Fitness Coaching
- `[Target Customer]` – Homeowners in Metro Heights, Small business owners, Busy parents
- `[Service Area / Location]` – Metro Heights suburbs, Atlantic County, Online / Nationwide
- `[Lead Magnet]` – Free Home Valuation, Free Plumbing Audit, Free 30-min Strategy Call, Free Website Audit
- `[Pain Point]` – Inconsistent leads from Zillow, High ad costs with low quality, Cold calling burnout
- `[Unique Benefit]` – Local expertise with zero pressure, Fast response + honest pricing, Results in 45 days or less
- `[Social Proof]` – 450+ families helped, 12 years experience, 4.98/5 rating, 187 homes sold in 2025
- `[Expected Leads]` – 8–15 / 20–35 / 50+ qualified leads per month
- `[Phone]`, `[Website]`, `[Email]`
- `[Tier Prices]` – Adjust to your market (real estate is higher ticket than many services)
- `[DM Script Context]` – Adapt the example script to your industry

## Tips for Any Industry
- **Local service businesses** (HVAC, plumbing, real estate, cleaning): Use phone + location in form. SMS is extremely effective.
- **Online/coaching/consulting**: Focus on email + calendar booking. Lead magnet can be a free guide, audit, or mini-course.
- **E-commerce**: Lead magnet = discount code or free sample. Capture email + "what are you looking for?"
- **B2B**: Longer sales cycle. Add "Company Size" and "Biggest Challenge" to form. Nurture is heavier on education.
